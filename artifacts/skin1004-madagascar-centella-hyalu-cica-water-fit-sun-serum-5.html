Verdict
Kesimpulan Ingredients
✓
3 modern UV filters (DHHB + EHT + MBBT) = solid protection. Semua photostable + broad spectrum. Kombinasi UVA + UVB coverage yang bagus.
✓
Niacinamide #8 = brightening bonus. Posisi cukup tinggi di sunscreen = konsentrasi meaningful. Brightening + anti-aging sambil protect.
✓
Triple HA hydration = dewy finish beneran. 3 bentuk HA (different molecular weights) buat multi-depth hydration. No wonder finish-nya dewy!
✓
Serum texture = lightweight. Chemical-only filters = no white cast. Perfect buat daily wear under makeup.
!
NOT alcohol free. Ada alcohol di formula. Kulit kering/sensitif yang reactive terhadap alcohol perlu hati-hati.
!
Chemical-only filters (no mineral). Semua UV filter chemical = bagus buat no white cast, tapi yang prefer mineral/hybrid filter perlu cari produk lain.
!
Centella 9800ppm = ~1% saja. Jangan expect efek centella sehebat cream centella dedicated. Ini lebih ke bonus soothing.
